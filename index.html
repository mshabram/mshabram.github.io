Megan I. Shabram

I am a NASA Postdoctoral Program Fellow at NASA Ames Research Center. I work on understanding planet formation by employing population inference techniques on data products from NASA's Kepler mission.

I received my PhD in Astrophysics at The Pennsylvania State University's Center for Exoplanets and Habitable Worlds in December 2015, my MSc in Astronomy at University of Florida in May 2012, and my BS in Physics at UC Santa Cruz in March 2009.
